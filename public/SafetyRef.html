Pilot Approval Standards

The standard for flying an OZ aircraft is deliberate and uncompromising.

Phase 01

Licence Verification

Validation of current PPL/CPL credentials and medical certifications directly with regulatory databases.

Phase 02

Document Review

Analysis of logbook history and currency requirements to ensure legal and operational compliance.

Phase 03

Experience Suitability

Evaluating specific flight hours in type and complex environment operations to match our fleet profile.

Phase 04

Checkout Induction

A mandatory flight with an OZ authorized instructor to verify handling and system knowledge.
